Vision and Project Overview

Reducing the cost and throughput of zkSNARKs on Ethereum

  • zkSNARKs are expensive today: they currently require requiring between 500k and 2 million gas per verification of a proof, which as of early November 2018 costs $0.50 or $2.00 at 5 Gwei/gas

  • We provide a 10x reduction in cost: our solution delegates the verification to a bonded third party which verifies the SNARK off-chain, and only posts the data to the main chain - thereby reducing the cost to verify a single zkSNARK proof to around 50k gas

  • Enables a new generation of applications: applications that were previously not economically feasible can now work - private ERC20 tokens, anonymous voting and many more
